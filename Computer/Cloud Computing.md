### **1. Explain cloud computing in one simple sentence.**

Cloud computing means **storing and accessing data and programs over the internet instead of your computer’s hard drive**.

---

### **2. Name one cloud service you have used and what you used it for.**

I have used **Google Drive** to **store and share documents online**.

---

### **3. How does cloud computing help a business save money?**

Cloud computing helps businesses save money by **reducing the need for expensive hardware and maintenance**, since everything runs online.

---

### **4. What does “scalability” mean in the context of the cloud?**

Scalability means **easily increasing or decreasing computing resources** (like storage or power) as needed.

---

### **5. What is the one essential thing you need to access your data in the cloud?**

You need an **internet connection** to access your data in the cloud.
